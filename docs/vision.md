# Expense Manager Vision

## Purpose

Expense Manager is a personal finance application that helps individuals track, understand, and improve their financial habits.

The application provides a simple and intuitive way to record income and expenses, categorize transactions, and view financial summaries. The application should also help users plan future expenses and financial goals over periods such as 1 year, 3 years, or 5 years.

## Problem Statement

Many people know how much they earn but do not know:

* Where their money is being spent
* Which categories consume the largest portion of their income
* Whether they are saving enough every month
* How their spending patterns change over time

Most existing solutions are either too complicated, require manual spreadsheet maintenance, or focus on accounting rather than personal finance.

## Target Users

* Individual users
* Families managing household expenses
* Students managing monthly budgets
* Professionals tracking income and spending

## Goals

* Make expense tracking fast and simple
* Provide meaningful insights into spending habits
* Help users stay within budgets
* Show trends over time
* Provide a clear view of financial health
* Allow users to create long-term financial plans
* Forecast future expenses and savings
* Help users prepare for major financial goals

## Non-Goals

The application is not intended to:

* Replace professional accounting software
* Handle business accounting
* Manage taxation or legal compliance
* Provide financial investment advice

## Success Criteria

A user should be able to:

1. Record an expense in less than 30 seconds
2. See monthly spending by category
3. Track income and expenses over time
4. Understand where money is going
5. Review financial history easily
6. Create a financial plan for 1, 3, or 5 years
7. Track progress toward financial goals
8. Compare planned expenses against actual expenses
